Cwmfest 22

Live at venues near you - Sept 23rd, 24th & 25th


Cwmfest is back! with a fantastic lineup for a weekend of live music across five venues, and a Family Fun Drum session at Cwmaman Arts Centre, we've got something for everyone


The festival was formed for the benefit of people of all ages who enjoy live music, and to promote Cwmaman and the surrounding areas. After a longer break than we hoped for due to the pandemic, the festival is now returning for its thirteenth year
